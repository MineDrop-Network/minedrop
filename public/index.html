Stealth System

No swords. Just skill.

MineDrop has zero traditional PvP. Instead, you infiltrate. You sneak. You read the base layout, time your entry, extract MineLings, and escape before anyone notices.

> INITIATING STEALTH PROTOCOL...
> TARGET: SkyKing's Base
> SCANNING defenses...
  [████████░░] 80%
> ENTRY POINT FOUND
> EXTRACTING Legendary 🔥...
> SUCCESS! +40x income secured
> ALARM TRIGGERED! RUN!
> █
  • 🔍 Recon first. Scout enemy bases and identify where high-value MineLings are placed before committing.
  • ⏱️ Time your entry. Bases have active and idle windows. Strike when the owner is offline or distracted.
  • 🎯 Precision extraction. Move too fast and alarms trigger. Move too slow and the owner logs on. Threading the needle is the skill.
  • 🛡️ Defend your stash. Lay traps, design confusing layouts, and set alarm tripwires to protect what you've built.
