Server Guidelines

MineDrop Network Rules

These rules exist to keep the server fair, competitive, and enjoyable for everyone. Read them. Know them. Play by them.

§01
Respect Everyone
  • No harassment, hate speech, or severe toxicity of any kind.
  • Friendly banter is allowed — but do NOT intentionally ruin the experience for others.
§02
No Cheating or Exploiting
  • Hacked clients, macros, dupes, and automation are strictly prohibited.
  • Exploiting bugs to gain an unfair advantage will result in a ban.
  • Found a bug? Report it to staff immediately — don't abuse it.
§03
Keep Chat Clean
  • No spam, NSFW content, or advertising other servers, services, or Discord servers.
§04
Fair Stealing & Raiding
  • Stealth raids and bounties are intended gameplay — use them strategically.
  • Exploiting bugs to bypass base protections is forbidden.
  • Real-money scams are strictly prohibited and will result in a permanent ban.
§05
Safe Trading
  • Use approved in-game trading systems only.
  • Cross-trading with other games or real money is NOT allowed under any circumstances.
§06
Account & Clan Responsibility
  • You are fully responsible for your own account and everything that happens on it.
  • Items lost due to account sharing will NOT be refunded — no exceptions.
  • Clan leaders are accountable for the actions of their members.
  • Alt-farming or coordinated exploits can lead to clan-wide punishments.
§07
Respect Staff & Common Sense
  • Staff decisions are final in serious situations — do not argue or harass staff members.
  • False reports or impersonating staff will lead to punishment.
  • If something gives an unfair advantage or harms the community, it is probably not allowed — use common sense.
// QUESTIONS OR DISPUTES

Reach out to staff on Discord for ban appeals, rule clarifications, or to report a violation. We're here to keep the game fair for everyone.
